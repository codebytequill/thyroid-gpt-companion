# Thyroid GPT Companion

**Thyroid GPT Companion** is a prototype personal knowledge system for organizing thyroid-related health notes, symptom tracking, supplement observations, lab questions, research findings, doctor visit preparation, and educational product development.

This project began as a no-code Notion workspace designed to reduce cognitive load and support pattern recognition, reflection, and research organization.

> **Important:** This project is for education, organization, journaling, and pattern tracking only. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition.

---

## Project Purpose

The goal of Thyroid GPT Companion is to create a warm, low-friction personal operating system for:

* tracking thyroid-related symptoms and observations
* organizing supplement notes and reassessment dates
* saving research cards and questions
* preparing for doctor visits
* identifying possible patterns over time
* supporting workbook and educational product development
* reducing mental clutter for users managing complex health information

---

## Core Design Principles

* Gentle, not clinical
* Educational, not diagnostic
* Low-friction and low-fatigue
* Pattern-focused rather than conclusion-driven
* Built for reflection, organization, and future product expansion
* Designed to support users who may feel overwhelmed by scattered notes, labs, research, and health questions

## Key Workspace Components

### Symptom Timeline

Tracks symptoms, energy, sleep, stress, possible triggers, and notes over time.

### Supplement Center

Organizes supplement name, dose, timing, goals, observed effects, concerns, reassessment dates, and related labs.

### Lab Explorer

Stores lab results, reference ranges, trends, questions, and notes for future review.

### Research Library

Captures research findings, sources, confidence levels, questions, tags, and possible product ideas.

### Doctor Visit Planner

Collects questions, priorities, appointment dates, answers, follow-ups, and visit preparation notes.

### Product Studio

Turns research and observations into workbook sections, educational posts, Etsy product ideas, lead magnets, and future ThyroidAI assets.

### Ask Thyroid GPT Command Center

A structured prompt launcher for tasks such as:

* summarizing trends
* reviewing supplements
* turning notes into doctor prep
* generating workbook pages
* organizing brain dumps
* transforming research into content
